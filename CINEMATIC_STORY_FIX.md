# Cinematic Story Fix - Coherence Over Spikiness

## Current Problem

The prompt pushes for "spiky insights" which creates:
- ❌ Disconnected shocking phrases
- ❌ Abstract, pretentious language
- ❌ No clear narrative thread
- ❌ Audience can't follow the story

**Example of Current Bad Output:**
```
Line 1: "Your identity is a fiction you tell yourself"
Line 2: "The mirror shows what others want to see"
Line 3: "Behind every mask is another mask"
Line 4: "Authenticity is the final illusion"
```
→ Abstract, confusing, no actionable insight

## What We Actually Need

1. **Clear Story Arc** - Beginning → Middle → End that makes sense
2. **Relevancy** - Addresses real problems audience faces
3. **Audience Connection** - Uses their language, their situations
4. **Concrete Examples** - Not abstract concepts

**Example of Good Story:**
```
Line 1: "Your parents saved ₹50 lakh in FDs over 30 years"
Line 2: "It grew to ₹1.2 crore. They felt safe."
Line 3: "The same amount in Nifty 50? ₹8.7 crore."
Line 4: "Playing it safe cost them ₹7.5 crore."
```
→ Clear, relatable, teaches something concrete

## Fix Strategy

### Priority 1: Story Coherence
- Each line must logically follow from the previous
- The 4 lines together tell ONE complete thought
- No random pivots or tangents

### Priority 2: Relevancy
- Address specific audience pain points
- Use real numbers and examples from verified data
- Avoid abstract philosophical statements

### Priority 3: Audience Language
- Write like a friend explaining over coffee
- Use their vocabulary, not marketing speak
- Specific situations they recognize

### Priority 4: Visual Storytelling
- Images support and enhance the narrative
- Visual metaphor creates continuity
- Each image advances the story
